Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 June 2026

What We Collect

We collect shop owner details, shop name, email, phone number, products, suppliers, sales records, compliance checklist items, and order notes entered into Spaza Shop OS.

Why We Use It

We use this information to run the shop book, show stock and sales reports, create WhatsApp order text, manage trial/payment status, and provide support.

South African Privacy

Spaza Shop OS is designed to follow POPIA-style privacy basics: collect only what is needed, keep it secure, explain why it is used, and allow users to request access, correction, export, or deletion.

Where Data Is Stored

During the pilot, data may be stored on servers outside South Africa. By using the service, you understand that your shop data may be processed cross-border for hosting and support.

Deletion and Export

You can request a copy of your data or ask for your shop account to be deleted. During the pilot, make the request through the same WhatsApp or email support channel used for setup.

Customer Information

Do not enter sensitive personal information into order notes. Only add the customer details needed to manage the order.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact the Spaza Shop OS operator through your setup/support channel.
