KasiStock
 
Brand Name

KasiStock

Why:
"Kasi" signals township/local business without sounding corporate.
"Stock" says the main value immediately.

Easy to say on WhatsApp, radio, Facebook, and in person.
Works beyond spaza shops later: tuck shops, small grocers, mobile sellers.
Slogan
Know your stock. Grow your shop.

Backup slogans:
Your shop book, on your phone.
Stock, sales, and reorders made simple.
See what sold. Know what to reorder.
One-Line Pitch

KasiStock is the R99/month shop book for spaza owners: stock, sales, expiry dates, supplier prices, and reorders from one phone.
